WHY SUBSCRIBE

For over 25 years, Katchafire have carried the sound of Aotearoa to festival stages, sold-out theatres, and living rooms across the globe — six albums deep, platinum-certified, and still delivering one of the most authentic roots reggae live shows on the planet. Behind every new single, every surprise tour stop, and every backstage moment is a global community that calls itself the Fyah Family, and this list is how you stay inside it.

Socials are great, but algorithms decide what you see and when, sometimes days after the news actually drops. The mailing list skips all of that. When a new song lands, when tickets go on pre-sale before the general public even knows a show exists, when merch restocks before it sells out again, or when the band drops a video shot straight from the road, Fyah Family subscribers hear about it first, straight to their inbox, no middleman.

This isn’t a marketing blast list, it’s the closest thing to being backstage. Subscribers get first access to pre-sale tickets for shows across Aotearoa, Australia, the US, and Europe, early word on festival lineups before they’re announced publicly, and the occasional surprise: unreleased demos, behind-the-scenes clips, and updates on the family that built this sound from a Hamilton apartment building into a movement that’s shared stages with Damian Marley, UB40, and Steel Pulse.

Katchafire built their name on authenticity: real roots reggae, real family, real shows, no shortcuts. The mailing list carries that same philosophy — no spam, no noise, just the updates that actually matter, delivered the moment they happen. Whether you’ve been riding with the Fyah Family since “Revival” dropped in 2003, or you just discovered the band through a festival set, this is the easiest way to make sure you’re never the last to know.

One sign-up, zero cost, and a direct line to the band. Drop your email above and become part of the Fyah Family today, the fire’s not going out anytime soon.
